Welcome to Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering at UGIP Nainital is dedicated to excellence in engineering education.

Established in 1992, our department has been producing highly skilled professionals who actively contribute to the infrastructure and society at large.

Our comprehensive curriculum is thoughtfully designed to meet the evolving demands of the modern industry, supported by state-of-the-art laboratories, highly experienced faculty, and strong industry collaborations.

👁️

Our Vision

To be a premier center of excellence in Civil Engineering, producing competent, innovative, and ethically grounded engineers dedicated to creating sustainable infrastructure for the nation.

Curriculum Overview

Our 3-year Diploma program spans across 6 semesters. The first year builds strong engineering fundamentals, leading into advanced specializations.

Sem 1English & Communication Skills
Sem 1Applied Mathematics – I
Sem 1Applied Physics – I
Sem 1Applied Chemistry – I
Sem 1Computer Fundamentals
Sem 2Applied Mathematics – II
Sem 2Environmental Science & Energy
Sem 2Engineering Graphics
Sem 2General Workshop Practice

Delve into specialized subjects covering advanced theory, hands-on practicals, comprehensive project work, and mandatory industrial training across Semesters 3 to 6, structured strictly as per the UBTER curriculum.
