Welcome to UGIP

Uttarakhand Government Institute of Polytechnic is a premier technical education institute in Nainital, established in 1956. We are committed to providing quality education and producing skilled professionals.

Our institute offers diploma programs in various engineering disciplines and pharmacy, with state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum.
